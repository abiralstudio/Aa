About Me

Hi, I’m Abiral Chettri from Gangtok, Sikkim.
At 23, I’ve spent the past 5 years living and breathing music—from the first beat to the final master, always chasing that perfect sound.
My main tool is FL Studio, but my real power comes from experience, creativity, and a love for making music that connects people.
Every song is a new story, and I put my knowledge, intuition, and heart into every mix and every beat.

My Philosophy & Process

Music is more than just sound—it's emotion, energy, and connection. My philosophy is simple: never settle for average.
I listen deeply to every project, collaborating with you to make sure your vision comes through—louder, clearer, and more powerful than you imagined.
Whether you want a hard-hitting beat, a clean and spacious mix, or your vocals to stand out, I use every trick in the book and a few of my own to get you there.

  • Personal Touch: I treat every project like it’s my own song, giving it time, detail, and honest feedback.
  • End-to-End Production: From songwriting ideas to final mastering—one studio, one vision.
  • Modern & Classic: Inspired by today’s sound but rooted in the timeless feel of classic music.
  • Community Driven: I love working with upcoming artists and believe in building each other up.
